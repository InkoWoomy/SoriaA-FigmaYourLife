About Alexander Soria
Alex Soria is a 20 year old video game enthusiast, who not only enjoys playing games, but also researching them, on both a story perspective and a programming perspective. His goal in the long term is to be a part of the video game industry, and is working to this goal with his passion for art and programming.

When he is not staring at screens, he is sometimes drawing, taking walks outside, or working part-time at his local Wendy's.
